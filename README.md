# Data Analytics Portfolio

## Summary
This portfolio showcases six data analytics projects that demonstrate skills in various tools including Excel, Power BI, SQL, Python, and Tableau.

---

### Projects

1. **Freight Forwarding Profitability Analysis**  
   - **Description**: Analyzed profitability across various shipping routes and customer segments.  
   - **Technologies Used**: Excel, SQL  
   - **Key Results**: Identified top-performing routes and customer segments, resulting in a 20% increase in profitability.

2. **Shipping Sales Analysis**  
   - **Description**: Conducted sales performance analysis over multiple quarters focusing on trends and patterns.  
   - **Technologies Used**: Power BI  
   - **Key Results**: Revealed seasonal trends, influencing inventory management and sales strategies.

3. **Hospital Patient Risk Analysis**  
   - **Description**: Evaluated patient data to identify high-risk patients and improve care plans.  
   - **Technologies Used**: Python, SQL  
   - **Key Results**: Achieved a 15% reduction in readmissions through targeted interventions.

4. **Crime Analysis**  
   - **Description**: Analyzed crime data to identify hotspots and trends over time.  
   - **Technologies Used**: Tableau  
   - **Key Results**: Provided actionable insights to local law enforcement, improving response times by 25%.

5. **Coffee Sales Analysis**  
   - **Description**: Analyzed sales data from various outlets to understand consumer preferences and trends.  
   - **Technologies Used**: Excel, Power BI  
   - **Key Results**: Developed targeted marketing strategies that increased sales by 30%.

6. **Freight Forwarding Top Customers**  
   - **Description**: Identified and analyzed the top customers in freight forwarding to optimize service offerings.  
   - **Technologies Used**: SQL, Python  
   - **Key Results**: Enhanced customer relations and retention by providing tailored services.
